Friction: <b>Friction</b> is a force that resists the motion of objects. 

Sliding: <b>Sliding</b> is moving smoothly over a surface.
                        
Force: A <b>force</b> is a push or pull that tends to make an object move, stop, or change direction.
                        
Surface: A <b>surface</b> is the outside of an object or body.
